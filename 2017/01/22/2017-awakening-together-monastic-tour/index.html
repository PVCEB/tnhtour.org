2017 Awakening Together Monastic Tour

Join the monastic community founded, ordained, and trained by Thich Nhat Hanh as they tour the United States to continue the teachings and practices of the beloved Zen master. Join us for retreats and days of mindfulness to practice and foster a collective awakening in ourselves and our world.
These are the planned dates for the 2017 Awakening Together Monastic Tour – stay tuned for more information and details in the coming weeks and months.
NEW YORK
August 23 – 27, 2017
Mindfulness Retreat – English
Blue Cliff Monastery
Pine Bush, New York
 
August 30 – September 3, 2017
Mindfulness Retreat – Vietnamese
Blue Cliff Monastery
Pine Bush, New York
CALIFORNIA
September 12 – 17, 2017
Mindfulness Retreat – English
Deer Park Monastery
Escondido, California
 
September 20 – 24, 2017
Mindfulness Retreat – Vietnamese
Deer Park Monastery
Escondido, California
TENNESSEE
October 7, 2017
Mindfulness Event
Memphis, Tennessee
MISSISSIPPI
October 8, 2017
Day of Mindfulness
Magnolia Grove Monastery
Batesville, Mississippi
 
October 10 – 15, 2017
Mindfulness Retreat
Magnolia Grove Monastery
Batesville, Mississippi
